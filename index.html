TADBIR – Privacy Policy

SJMMJ  •  Last updated: June 2026

TADBIR is a community management application developed for SJMMJ Nadiad. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name, phone number, and city provided during registration
  • Gender and kabila (community group) information
  • Device token for push notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • App usage data stored in Firebase Firestore

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to community notices, events, and announcements
  • To send relevant push notifications based on your city and role
  • To manage event participation requests and library book issues
  • To display your profile within the app

3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Cloud Messaging). Firebase complies with GDPR and other international data protection standards. We do not store data on any third-party servers outside of Firebase.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only accessible to authorized administrators of SJMMJ Nadiad.

5. Push Notifications

TADBIR uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications about community events, notices, and updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

TADBIR is intended for use by community members aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase security rules and encrypted data transmission to protect your personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
