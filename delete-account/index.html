SJMMJ Nadiad  •  Account Deletion Request

Account deletion in TADBIR is managed by the SJMMJ Nadiad administration team. To request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us directly.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to info@hikmahvision.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered phone number. Our admin team will process your request within 7 working days.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your name, phone number, city, and profile information
  • Your event participation requests
  • Your book issue history
  • Your FCM device token

What Data May Be Retained

  • Anonymous usage logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security purposes
